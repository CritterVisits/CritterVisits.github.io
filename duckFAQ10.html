When you get your notice that we have your booking ready, you will get a PDF with protocols for arrival, as well as a map with specific directions.
Upon arrival, you will drive up the laneway to Woolley Wonderland Farm.
On Duck Days, there may be a line-up of cars. Please wait in your vehicle, moving up with the line. It won’t take very long.
We have a team on site who will greet you.
When approached by our team member, let them know that you are here to pick-up your foster duckling kit. Â If you have an urgent washroom need, let them know as and they will direct you to the facilities right away.
Staff will meet you near our garage and direct you to back up to one of the bays. You will tell them the name the order was placed, hand them your duckie box for the trip home and someone will prepare your ducklings.
Meanwhile, you will get out of your vehicle to load your kit items into your vehicle. The kit consists of a large bale of shavings in plastic, a bag or bucket of food weighing approx. 12 kg. and our “loot bag” With your instruction manual, food and water dishes as well as the “duck gatorade”. If you ordered a HEAT LAMP or CAGE we will get that for you as well. If you forgot to order a heat lamp, we can take your payment on the spot and provide you with one on loan.
Your ducklings require heat and no drafts so it is important to get them into your vehicle immediately. If you have time, you can pull aside and say hi to the animals in our paddocks.
