Answer...

Ducks poop …  a lot. Every 15 minutes apparently.
Fecal matter can be runny or lumpy depending if it’s Poo or Pee. Duck feces is directly related to what they have eaten, as they have a fast metabolism.  What goes in comes out relatively quickly. Their poop will reflect what they eat. Do not panic if a tomato  eaten turns the poop a strange colour.

Birds have 3 types of feces...Urates are the nitrogenous waste, are white, related to the amount of protein metabolized and variable of the amount in any defecation. Cecal feces tend to be liquid and smelly from the cecal fermentation and not related to recent diet. Intestinal feces are the undigested food.

To minimize mess you can: keep them in their brooder, use a kiddie pool (dry) with shavings in the bottom for more room. Build cardboard sides perhaps or fashion a gate around with chicken wire OR follow them around with a wipe!
