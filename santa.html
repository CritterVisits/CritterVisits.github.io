Santa and Sleigh at the Farm

We’ve opened our Farm, and Santa has agreed to come and visit with you for just a few dates before his big night!
Our online booking will show you available appointments on Saturdays, Sundays and the week of Christmas too.

Arrive for your Farm Family Time and our Elves will be on hand to guide you. Our “Welcome Elf” will check you in and collect your contact information (for contact tracing with Covid regulations) before escorting your group to meet the “Jolly Old Elf“ himself!

Santa and his “reindeer/pony” Angus will greet you at our photo-friendly area. Decorated trees, a rocking horse, cozy chair and the North Pole are settled into a bed of straw for the rustic feel of a Barnyard Christmas. We’ll be asking in advance for your kiddo’s names, ages and other details, to make the visit real for them as Santa has a chat with your children. Angus can be brushed and petted as part of this time.
A Professional Photographer will be on site to offer their services (additional fees from $20 will apply if you choose to use their expertise. You will arrange payment with them, and digital images will be sent to you directly.
You are welcome to take all the video footage and photos during the 15 minutes with Santa, who has a small gift for the kiddos too!

Our Sleigh Ride zone in our front field where Meadow and Oreo take turns pulling our red or gold sleighs. They will do laps around the field for a One-Pony-Open-Sleigh ride. We’ve had chorus’ of Jingle Bells spontaneously sung as kiddos are pulled in the Sleigh. We can fit three littles, a grown-up and a little, or just a pair of grown-ups for each ride. During this 15-minute time, you will continue taking turns, allowing everyone who wishes to have a ride.

With a good-bye to your new Elf friends, and head back to your car for a safe trip home.

Fees: $75 per family of up to 6 (they must be from the same social bubble). HST is applied to all purchases.
Re-read the full description again on the booking site. There are no refunds or changes allowed.

For a personal family experience at your location; Santa, Elves and the sleigh can come to you.
