The automatic tuner gently rolls the eggs so there is no need to turn them by hand. It will stop turning two days before the hatch date to let those eggs settle in time for the pipping out. LOCKDOWN allows you to get the brooder box ready as the last 2 days of the hatch are here.

Pipping and peeping time when the egg tooth breaks a tiny hole in the shell. The chick gets to the tough job of pushing it's way out of the egg.

The newborn chick can last up to 24 hours in the incubator before it is moved to your brooder.
