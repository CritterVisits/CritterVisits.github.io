Our foster program is currently set up for pickup at the farm.

It is about a 4 hr return trip from Toronto to Woolley Wonderland Farm in Lakehurst.

However, we do have an experinced duck delivery service available to pickup your ducklings and kit on your confimred pickup day and bring them to your location if it is in or around the Greater Toronto Area (GTA).

The no-contact delivery cost depends on your location. For a delivery quote, please email to Lorne at DuckDelivery@bell.net.
